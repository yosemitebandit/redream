About ReDream.Us

Relive your dreams with ReDream

ReDream builds dream montages from vimeo's archival footage. Describe your dream and our diligent workers will synthesize an audiovisual experience based on the key moments of your memory.

Our system makes use of NLTK, Popcorn.js and many other libraries.
